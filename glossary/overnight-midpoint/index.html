What is Overnight Midpoint?

The 50% level of the overnight range — the session's equilibrium, marked in White at 6:25 AM.

The DefinitionOvernight Midpoint, defined

The Overnight Midpoint is the exact 50% level between the overnight high and overnight low, marked in White during the 6:00 AM Protocol. It serves as the session's equilibrium line: above it, price is in premium (look for shorts); below it, price is in discount (look for longs). It's also a gravitational level — in ranging conditions, price returns to the midpoint repeatedly, making it both a target and a decision line.

Why It MattersWhy Overnight Midpoint matters

The midpoint is the quickest bias check in trading. One glance tells you whether you're hunting longs or shorts this morning.

In PracticeOvernight Midpoint — a real example

Example · New York Session

ON range 5,828–5,848 → midpoint 5,838. Price opens at 5,842, runs to 5,846, stalls. Above midpoint = premium. You wait for a short Reveal instead of chasing longs.

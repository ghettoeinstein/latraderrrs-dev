What is Discipline?

Executing the process exactly — especially when you don't feel like it. The meta-skill that contains all others.

The DefinitionDiscipline, defined

Discipline in trading is the consistent execution of a defined process regardless of emotional state: marking the same levels at the same time every morning, taking only A+ setups, sizing every trade by the same formula, honoring every stop, and walking away when the window closes. Discipline isn't a personality trait — it's an environment: checklists, rules, and structure that make the right action automatic and the wrong action impossible. That's why the LA Traders system is built as a protocol, not a set of suggestions.

Why It MattersWhy Discipline matters

Everyone knows what to do. Discipline is doing it on the day you just lost 2R, the FOMO candle is ripping without you, and your brain is screaming for action.

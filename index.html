The Filter

Three questions before every trade

The RRR framework isn't a signal service — it's the lens every setup has to pass through before risk goes on.

01 / REVEAL
What did the market reveal?
Price sweeps a level, breaks structure, or rejects a zone — showing you which side is in control before you risk anything.
02 / RETRACE
Where should it retrace?
Direction rarely runs in a straight line. Identify the level it should pull back to, then wait for that zone to be tested.
03 / RUN
Where is it likely to run?
With the reveal confirmed and the retrace respected, enter with the move — targeting the next level of liquidity.

How This Fits the RRR Framework

Position sizing happens after the Reveal and before the Retrace completes. The Protocol requires you to know your size before you enter — never after. Calculate your risk, round down to whole contracts, and only enter if the risk-to-reward is 2:1 or better. Learn position sizing →
